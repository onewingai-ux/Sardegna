Victory Point Mapping Tool

Each province has a Laurel wreath on it that says how many VP 1st and 2nd place gets (e.g. "4 / 2" or "6 / 3").

Instructions:
1. Click a Red P-Dot on the map.
2. Type in the VP values printed in the laurel for that region.
3. Repeat for all 16 regions.
