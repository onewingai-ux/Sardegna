Fort Mapping Tool

In Sardegna, there are 19 fort locations (crossroads marked with numbers 1 through 19).

For the rules engine to know which fort gives influence to which province, we need a map showing what provinces each fort touches.

Click on a fort number on the map, then click the provinces that touch that fort. Click the fort again to finish it and move to the next fort. Do this for all 19 forts, starting from 1 to 19.
