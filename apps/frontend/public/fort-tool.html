Fort Mapping Tool

In Sardegna, there are 26 fort locations (crossroads marked with numbers 1 through 26).

For the rules engine to know which fort gives influence to which province, we need a map showing what provinces each fort touches.

Instructions:
1. Click on a numbered fort crossroad.
2. Click directly on the Red P-Dots that touch that fort.
3. Click the cyan "Done" button at the bottom right when you've selected all touching provinces.
4. Repeat for all 26 forts.
