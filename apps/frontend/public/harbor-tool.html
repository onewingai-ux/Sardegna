Harbor Mapping Tool

The rules mention harbors on the edge of the board where ships can be placed.

Instructions:
1. Click the map to place Harbor 1.
2. Click directly on the Red P-Dots that touch that harbor (so the engine knows which province gets the ship's influence).
3. Click the cyan "Done" button at the bottom right.
4. Repeat for all harbors.
